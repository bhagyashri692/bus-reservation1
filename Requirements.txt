To make a bus reservation, you'll need to provide the following information: 
Departure and arrival points: The location of where the bus will leave from and where it will arrive
Date and time: The day and time of the bus's departure and return (if applicable)
Number of passengers: The total number of people traveling, including infants
Bus Booking Software: Simplify Your Transportation Management
You can make bus reservations online using a bus reservation system. These systems are available on mobile or web and allow users to create a profile and book tickets.
